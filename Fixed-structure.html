Three restaurants for your San Francisco to Eugene road trip

Considering a road trip between San Francisco, California and Eugene, Oregon? Here are three food options on the way that have been thoroughly tested.

In-N-Out Burger

In-N-Out is a fast food staple of the West Coast. The one in Vacaville, California is always a reliable option. I recommend a Double-Double, fries, and pink lemonade.

Yaks Shack

Yaks Shack has an eccentric selection of burgers and sandwiches. Yaks Shack in Mount Shasta, California is a great place to eat a large amount of food with a view of the volcano. I recommend the curly fries.

Sammich

Sammich has excellent sandwiches with an emphasis on house-made ingredients. Located in Ashland, Oregon, Sammich is a great place to relax with an artisinal sandwich and enjoy the scenery. Also, sandwiches come with chips as a bonus. I recommend whatever the special is.
