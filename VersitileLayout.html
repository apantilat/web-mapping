Activities in Washington, DC

There is more to Washington, DC than politicians. This interactive map shows some of the ways to enjoy America's capitol city. Whether you are interested in theatre, music, bars, or bicycles, this map has something for everyone.

Explore the map and zoom in on an activity. Click on an activity icon for more information. Toggle the visible activites with the blue buttons in the top-right.

Theatre

There are many great theatres in Washington, DC for drama enthusiasts. Each one offers a different experience.

Bar

Bars are a classic place to unwind in Washington, DC. This one near Arlington National Cemetery is one you can't miss.

Bicycle

Tired of the city? Washington DC, has excellent bicycle routes to get some fresh air. This one near Rock Creek is a local favorite.

Music

Washington, DC is home to many great music venues. Wherever you are, there is probably one nearby. See one of your favorite artists or discover something new.
